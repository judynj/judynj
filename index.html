joining the Student Union

Posted on 2020-11-12 |

Last week,I heard that the Student Union was looking for new members. After hearing the news, I enrolled immediately.

The interview came on Tuesday noon. I was really nervous when entering the room, then I stood on platform,while there was eight to nine students in front of me ,including the chairman and two vice-chairmen,and four ministers of four departments,which are known as activity department, IT department, financial department and academic department.

I introduced myself and told them that my experience was rich enough to be a member of activity department, because I was student in charge of propaganda work, and the work I did was associated with this department, such as decorating board and class cultural board , buying props that was needed in the sport meeting. They asked me what work could I take on if I was chosen, and I answered that I could organize some activities,since I did them so well before.

Soon I was informed that I was chosen in the afternoon, the ministers gave me congratulation,but at the same time she said I could talk more next time.

The advice made me think a lot. I am not a person that like talking much since I was very young. I was the student that spending the least time interviewed by those people because of the short speech. The phenomenon shows that I need a longer time to react after getting the question, as a result, I do not have many things to say. After thinking about it , I realize maybe the experience I have is not rich enough for me to come up with an example immediately when interviewed. However, that could be improved as time goes by.

continuing our experiment in IYPT

Posted on 2020-11-04 |

We made a lot of conversations after getting the hard topic. Finally our team gave up the experimental variable value that was talked about before because the information related to this was too hard for us to understand. Finally ,we started our experiment about the relationship between the rotate speed and the height it reached .

Then here came a question about the facility,the equipment we found on taobao turned out to be useless. Therefore we made one by ourselves,and the material came from daily life,for example,the engine came from an electric fan. After comparing many ways,we decided to fix the hand helicopter on the engine by hot melt glue gun. The process was amazing ,and that was my first time using such a machine.

There came another question: How can we count the times when it was rotating and how to measure the height?Our tutor recommended an app ,but it was complex to use. It came out that the result was amazing，and the times it rotated could be counted clearly.

From this experience,I learned a lot about how to choose a proper app to help analyze the data and how to use a machine that I had never used before. The two hours flew away quickly. In the end ,we not only recorded the experimental data,but also gained happiness and friendship between each other.

However it turns out to be difficult. After several sessions, I found it easy to keep balance on ice ,but it was hard to skate graceful or as pretty as my coach did. In addition,sometimes I couldn’t match up my waist with legs, so the center of gravity changed a lot during coasting.

Diving is a extreme sport so they are many things I should learn,like the equipment,the safety tips after getting deep in the sea.

Here are some pictures when I first learned how to dive into the water., the first step is great,and there is still a long way to go.

Besides these,I will start to join aerobics next week,and learn to play table tennis by myself with my classmates. Those kinds of sports make my spare time colorful.

activities in Mock Trial

Posted on 2020-10-20 |

Recently I spent a lot of time on writing DX（known as direct examinations) for one of my clubs called Mock Trial. We will have a competition with students in Nanjing Foreign Language School.

The process writing DX is really exciting .We got a case summary about a girl named Jordan,who was good at running but died because of hooking EPO under too much pressure. After her death,her parents charged a crime upon Coach Swift,who was major suspect that caused her death. Our duty was acted as different roles in these case and debated to find who was the actual murder.

I chose to act as Jordon’s friend,Morgan Pearce,a girl that knew the whole process. My partner I wrote DX with is my classmate Estalla. She acted as my attorney.

At first both of us had no idea what a DX was,so we looked through the many models the got the point,and that took us much time. Then the next problem came. Both of us were too busy to write DX together in school. The problem was dealt with quickly after a short time conversation. We came up with an appointed time to make a call on the wechat,then completed the task as soon as possible. That very evening she went online on time.

Since the DX has a format as a key to a question,we decided to divide our work. Estalla and I first confirmed the questions she would ask through the phone call,then she typed her them on WPS,at the same time I sent her my answer to the questions on the wechat for her to copy. The method was efficiency,and we done most of the work in one and a half hours.

The process of our work is like this.

Here is my feeling after finishing the task:

It is really my pleasure to cooperate with such a good girl. Estalla is one of the most excellent students I have ever seen before. She is easy and friendly to communicate with,and she is willing to finish the DX efficiently and perfectly as I thought we should. During our cooperating,both she and I was happy to accept suggestions from each other. This is not my first time cooperating with Estalla,and I hope that we could be partner again some day.

Anyway,choosing a good partner before starting a project is really important!

the feeling after attending FBLA

Posted on 2020-10-06 |

​ The feeling after attending FBLA

​ 参加FBLA社团的感想

Recently,I have been a member of a club set in our school called FBLA. Our first task is setting a virtual company to provide goods for the coming Food Festival in January next year.

入学后我加入了一个叫作FBLA的社团.我们小组的第一个任务是创建一个虚拟的公司为明年一月份的校园美食节做准备.

The activity sounds great ,but we actually meet a lot of problems. Our group has six girls,but at first when we discussed the name of the group on wechat ,I found some of girls were too shy to come up with ideas,so they kept silence. Our group leader felt annoyed about it,so she sent some words in the wechat group,mainly to encourage the teammates to be more active and tell us their thoughts. That really worked. The goods we would produce were soon decided,so did Plan ABC. Yesterday a questionnaire about the advice the students in our school would like to give us was also done. Anyway ,the work we do together is more and more efficient.

这个活动听上去非常有意思，但实际上我们遇到了很多问题。我们小组的组员是六个女孩，但我发现一开始谈论小组名称的时候，一些女孩因为含羞从不提出建议。组长有点恼火，然后在微信群里发了些消息，大概意思就是想让组员们畅所欲言。这方法奏效了。我们小组很快确定了商品并制作了方案ABC。就在昨天，为了调查全校同学对方案的建议，一份电子版调查问卷新鲜出炉。我们越来越高效了。

Here is the feeling about the teamwork.

以下是我对这次活动的初步感受。

  • Be active and always share thoughts when solving problems in a group,or the efficiency would be particularly low when all the teammates keep silence.

在集体活动中保持活跃，保持沉默只会让工作变得低效。

**

  • Be organized and divide the duty to each of the members. If several people deal with one together,that maybe a waste of time because everyone has a different idea,and the problem cannot be solved in a short period of time.

分配任务可以让工作变得更有条理。如果一群人做同一个任务，人多口杂必然会浪费时间。

**

  • Be manful as our group leader did. Because she pointed out of other’s mistakes, they can be corrected as soon as possible ,and then avoid the waste of more time.

要学习组长的果断。她及时指出了组员的错误并让她们立刻改正，这样很好地避免了浪费更多的时间

Hope we can finish the task successfully in the next four months!

希望我们可以一起在接下来的四个月里顺利完成任务！

Activities in IYPT

Posted on 2020-09-28 |

I have been in a club about physics called IYPT since the new semester started. The project our group focus on is about hand helicopter,which is a big challenge for us.

刚入学就参加了叫作IYPT的物理社团。我们小组研究的主题是竹蜻蜓（手提直升机），对于我们来说是一个巨大的挑战。

*The theme is like this: a simple hand helicopter can be made by attaching rotor blades to one end of a vertical stick. The helicopter moves upwards when the stick is twisted at a high enough speed and let go. Investigate how the relevant parameters affect the lift-off and the maximum height.**

题目是这样的：一架简单的手提直升机可以通过将旋翼叶片固定在垂直杆的一端制作而成。当垂直杆以足够高的速度旋转时，松开手，手提直升机就会起飞。探究相关参数对起飞和飞行最大高度的影响。

All of our group member feel confused about the project when we first read it .The area it is involved is far beyond our level. we made a plan of clear division of work,then discussed the type of hand helicopter we should buy on the Internet. Later we searched essays in both Chinese and English ,most of them are written by college students,then learn the professional formula and words by ourselves. These work was all done in after-class time. Then in the club time of second week,we did a couple groups of contrastive experiment and got some useful data.

所有的组员都对这个课题感到无比困惑，因为它超出了我们的知识范围。很快我们做了详细的分工，然后讨论应该在网上买什么样类型的竹蜻蜓。然后我们又去搜索了大学生写的关于空气动力学的学术论文，并自学其中的专业公式和词汇。这些都是在课余时间做的事情。在第二次的社团课上，我们做了几组对比实验并得到了有用的数据。

These are the whole process since these two weeks. Although we did not do too much,but the confusion when first reading the theme is gone , and the experimental goal is more clearly .We all gain valuable experience –the awareness of the important of teamwork,for any wrong division of work will absolutely leads to failure.

这些是两周以来IYPT社团的进展。虽然工作量不是很大，但是看到课题时的一头雾水已经几乎消散，实验目标渐渐明晰。我们也从中得到了宝贵的经验——意识到了团队合作的重要，因为任何一个人工作量太大或者太小都会影响事情的成功与失败。

Hope my group will be success step by step.

希望我的小组一步步走向成功。

TO myself

Posted on 2020-09-20 |

To myself:

致三年后的我：

I can not predict what you will be in the next three years，but express my expectations to you instead.Three years are neither too long nor too short,however it may change you totally.After three years when you read this letter,let us check whether you become the one you wanna be three years ago.

我无法预料这三年间发生了什么，只能在此表达下对你的期许。三年说不长也不长，说不短也不短，也许它会使你有一个脱胎换骨的蜕变。三年过去，你看看，有没有变成三年前我想要的样子。

I hope you can be a member of your ideal university.Whether your goals will change and how many times it will be changed is unknown ,but please work hard to chase your dream.Wish you won’t let those who cares about you all the time down,and have a bright future which you have dreamed of.

愿你你能考上目标的大学。不知道你的目标在这三年间有没有发生变化，会变化几次，但请一定要好好努力，愿你以梦为马，不负众望，未来可期。

I hope you can be more confident in the next three years.Make more friends and join more clubs you interested in .Wish you get everything you want and full of energy.

愿你在这三年里做一个自信的自己。多交些朋友，加入社团。愿你的未来有求必应，不缺实力。

I hope you can be more maturer after this three years period of time .I know you deal with things irritably from time to time.Wish you be kinder to others and apologize when you do something wrong.

愿三年后的你能更加成熟。三年前的你总是暴躁的处理事情，希望以后的你，平和待人,即使伤着人了也要说对不起

I hope you can be proud of yourself and smile from the first to the end.Your aims will be more and more clearly.Don’t hesitate when meeting multiple choices ,just make a decision and walk towards.Hope you will have a pupil to depend on when tired,have the ability to handle all the situations,have a positive attitude when facing hard times.Moreover,always be gentle while be treated gently.

愿三年后的你，甘之如饴，所得归于欢喜。要坚信你的未来会越来越明晰。如果遇到了岔路口，也别摇摆不定，选择一条路义无反顾的走下去吧。但愿未来的你疲有所依，有高跟鞋也有球鞋，风雨后心上没有补丁。在温柔待人的同时也要被人温柔以待。

Last but not the least,I hope you balance your habits while complete your schoolwork perfectly.Now I keep only a few minutes’ enthusiasm sometime.But time flies,nobody knows what you will be like,including yourself.

愿你漂亮的完成学业同时兼顾自己的爱好，现在的我是一个三分钟热度的人。但时间还长，谁知道这三年间你会变成什么样呢？

I am still confused about my school life ,homework,friendship and so many things when writing this .No matter what decisions you make —-I am always here accompanying with you ,as past memory lives forever.

现在给你写信的我还在苦苦挣扎的迷茫期。不论你今后做出什么样的决定——我一直都陪伴着你，因为我是你的回忆。

Hope you are still the girl you want to be when you reread this letter in the future, with honor and heart that remains the same.

愿你归来，仍是少年。
