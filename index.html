British educational system

Posted on 2020-11-23 |

I did a research about British educational system recently. Here is my mind map for the research.

Here is my research.

Britain has a long history of educating. Its education system has undergone hundreds of years of evolution, which is quite complete and complex, and has great flexibility. Generally speaking, It is divided into three stages :Compulsory Education, Further Education, and Higher Education. The differences between Chinese and British education systems make it impossible for students of the two countries to match each other in terms of age and academic level. Therefore, when considering studying in the UK, Chinese students must fully understand the differences between the two education systems and accurately determine where their level is in the UK education system. In order to choose appropriate courses and arrange reasonable study plan according to their own characteristics.

\Compulsory Education**

●The first stage is from 5-7 years old called the kindergarten education.

●The second stage is from 7-11 years old called primary education.

●The third stage is from 11-16 years old called secondary education.

The period of compulsory education prescribed by law is between the ages of 5 and 16. School-age children must attend school, and the State is responsible for the necessary tuition fees, books and necessary supplies.

The GCSE is the diploma obtained after completing the last two years of Secondary Education. The number of Chinese students studying at GCSE is small, and is only suitable for students under 16.

\Methods of marking**

Grade A* (A good result of exceptional quality)

Grade A (The higher grades needed for further and higher education)

Grade B

Grade C

Grade D (Regarded as average grades-a good base from which to grow)

Grade E

Grade F (The lower, but still useful,grades of achievement )

Grade G

Ungraded (Unfortunately, no score )

●**The fourth stage (further education)**

16-18 years old is a senior middle school or college preparatory class, which is the transition period from middle school to university, also known as continuing education stage, mainly includes academic direction and career direction study. Students who complete the preparatory courses mainly take the General Certificate of Education (Advanced Level).In addition to take the GCE-A level, vocational school students take the GNVQ (General National Vocational Qualification).

\Further education**

Further education is the most exciting part of the UK education system. It is also a crucial stage for Chinese high school students to study in the UK. Students in further education generally fall between Grade 8 and Grade 9. It is divided into two systems: the academic route and the vocational route. Both systems are equally valued in Britain.

\Academic route:(A******-level**\)\

The full name is General Certificate of Education Advanced Level, which is an important stage of the academic course. There are more than 100 subjects in it. We tend to think of A-level as the basic course of university. Generally subjects are selected according to two principles, which are known as professional objective principle and proficiency principle.

\professional objective principle**

The three or four A-level subjects should be chosen around the major that the student wishes to study at undergraduate Level and his/her future career goals.

\proficiency principle**

A-level grades also determine where student gets into university, so make sure you take one or two subjects that you are good at.

\vocational route**

GNVQ is similar to the GCSE and A-level in the academic route. Students can arrange courses according to their own needs and interest,and can even work in the process of learning.

\The elective system**

The elective system is simple and is divided into three parts,the Foundation, the Intermidiate(is similar as 4 sessions in GCSE and generally takes one year to complete), the Advanced(is equal to 2 A-levels and generally takes two years t complete)

\Methods of marking**

Distinction =A/B

Merit=C

Pass=D/E

●**The fifth stage**

It is called the college education, generally from the age of 18. It takes 3-4 years (medical for 5 years)for undergraduate study to obtain a bachelor’s degree. A master’s degree usually lasts 1-2 years,and a doctor’s degree usually lasts 3-5 years.

\A bachelor’s degree**

Bachelor Degree includes BA-Bachelor of Art，BSc-Bachelor of Science，BEng-Bachelor of Engineering,LLB

\University of joint school**

It is similar to the situation in China where many universities have secondary schools attached to them. Joint schools are generally close in geographical location,usually located in the same city. The purpose of establishing joint schools is to complement each other in teaching facilities and curriculum arrangement.

The process writing DX is really exciting .We got a case summary about a girl named Jordan,who was good at running but died because of hooking EPO under too much pressure. After her death,her parents charged a crime upon Coach Swift,who was major suspect that caused her death. Our duty was acted as different roles in these case and debated to find who was the actual murder.

the feeling after attending FBLA

Posted on 2020-10-06 |

​ The feeling after attending FBLA

​ 参加FBLA社团的感想

Recently,I have been a member of a club set in our school called FBLA. Our first task is setting a virtual company to provide goods for the coming Food Festival in January next year.

入学后我加入了一个叫作FBLA的社团.我们小组的第一个任务是创建一个虚拟的公司为明年一月份的校园美食节做准备.

The activity sounds great ,but we actually meet a lot of problems. Our group has six girls,but at first when we discussed the name of the group on wechat ,I found some of girls were too shy to come up with ideas,so they kept silence. Our group leader felt annoyed about it,so she sent some words in the wechat group,mainly to encourage the teammates to be more active and tell us their thoughts. That really worked. The goods we would produce were soon decided,so did Plan ABC. Yesterday a questionnaire about the advice the students in our school would like to give us was also done. Anyway ,the work we do together is more and more efficient.

这个活动听上去非常有意思，但实际上我们遇到了很多问题。我们小组的组员是六个女孩，但我发现一开始谈论小组名称的时候，一些女孩因为含羞从不提出建议。组长有点恼火，然后在微信群里发了些消息，大概意思就是想让组员们畅所欲言。这方法奏效了。我们小组很快确定了商品并制作了方案ABC。就在昨天，为了调查全校同学对方案的建议，一份电子版调查问卷新鲜出炉。我们越来越高效了。

Here is the feeling about the teamwork.

以下是我对这次活动的初步感受。

  • Be active and always share thoughts when solving problems in a group,or the efficiency would be particularly low when all the teammates keep silence.

在集体活动中保持活跃，保持沉默只会让工作变得低效。

**

  • Be organized and divide the duty to each of the members. If several people deal with one together,that maybe a waste of time because everyone has a different idea,and the problem cannot be solved in a short period of time.

分配任务可以让工作变得更有条理。如果一群人做同一个任务，人多口杂必然会浪费时间。

**

  • Be manful as our group leader did. Because she pointed out of other’s mistakes, they can be corrected as soon as possible ,and then avoid the waste of more time.

要学习组长的果断。她及时指出了组员的错误并让她们立刻改正，这样很好地避免了浪费更多的时间

Hope we can finish the task successfully in the next four months!

希望我们可以一起在接下来的四个月里顺利完成任务！
